Class of aircraft heavier than air.

Airplane is a class of heavier-than-air aircraft designed to fly in the atmosphere with the help of a power unit that creates thrust and a wing that is stationary relative to other parts of the apparatus, creating lift.

5 Interesting facts:

  1. In case you didn't know, most of the time the plane is controlled by an autopilot. During this time, pilots eat, play chess, watch DVDs and do whatever they want. Why be tired if you don't have to be, right?
  2. In the air, the pilot is king and God. He is not only responsible for takeoff and landing, but also controls everything that happens on board. The pilot can arrest the violator, issue a fine and even certify the will. So don’t even think about pissing off the pilot-in-command – after landing you will face serious punishment.
  3. No, it is not needed to calm the offender. It's simple: an ax is included in the list of emergency equipment. With its help, you can put out a fire, open a stuck door, or cut a way out in case of an emergency. There are even marks on the fuselage where it is better to make an emergency “door”.
  4. As experienced pilots say, lightning striking a ship is not at all uncommon. Airplanes are made so that a flash cannot disable them. Passengers during lightning hear a strong roar and nothing more: the flight continues on the given course.
  5. When a plane lands at night, flight attendants dim the interior lights. In the unlikely event that the plane lands poorly and passengers have to evacuate, their eyes will have had time to adjust to the darkness.
